How to make a sandwich:

  1. Gather ingredients such as bread, protein, cheese, lettuce and preferred condiments.
  2. Get 2 slices of bread and place them next to one another on a plate.
  3. Place cheese and preferred protein on one slice of bread.
  4. Add lettuce to your sandwich.
  5. Add preffered condiments to your sandwhich.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top and enjoy your sandwich.
